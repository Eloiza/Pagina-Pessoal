About

I am a student of Biomedical Informatics undergraduate course in UFPR. Currently, I am in the second year of "scientific initiation" as a member of Visão Robótica e Image (VRI) Lab in Department of Informatics. My research project consists of developing neural networks to assist the diagnosis of the HER2 exam based on patients slides. This exam is required to choose the best treatment for patients diagnosed with breast cancer. This project started with a master's research work from another student in the same lab. My interests include the fields of machine learning and data science.
